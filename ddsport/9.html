Movies DD Sports

DD Sports app has a huge library of movies. From latest releases to the old classics. From international blockbusters such as the Avengers to lesser-known domestic films.

However, the movies may not be available in all regions, due to copyright infringement.

The movies tab is systematically subdivided into categories such as Action, comedy, for kids, popular, new on DD Sports, Biopic and so on.

Interestingly; based on your previous choices, the app also provides a subcategory called top picks for you.
