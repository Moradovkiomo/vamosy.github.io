Play DD Sports

The most recent update of the DD Sports App came with a new feature. With cricket events such as IPL and ICC cricket world cup approaches, the app offereda unique experience to the user.

The viewers, if not on full-screen mode, could see certain options on the bottom of their screens.

These options are the likely occurrences on the next balL, 1/2/3 or 4/6 or wicket or no/wide ball.

Correctly predicting the outcome grants the user points. The more unlikely the outcome the higher the points. Each over serves as a round and at the end of each round, the scores are compiled.

Multipliers are also added on consecutive correct guesses.
