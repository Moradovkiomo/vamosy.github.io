Sport DD Sports

Star has rights to cover almost all major sporting events occurring around the world.

Some are covered live simultaneously, such as in the case of soccer; when multiple teams play at the same time.

DD Sports app has a sports option for you, to skip all the content and jump right into your favorite sport.

The live and most popular events are listed on the top.

The sports tab also provides the option of viewing the highlights of any match or event you might have missed. Multiple events of the same sports may occur at the same time.

So the tab has sub-categories in the form of the name of the sport, like cricket, football, etc.
