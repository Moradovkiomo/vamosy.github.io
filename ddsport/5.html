Wishlist DD Sports

With more producers focusing to put their work on exclusive display, new weekly episodes and much-anticipated content might be lost to the user. Do not worry, DD Sports app got you covered.

The application provides an option to add the content to a wish list. This will allow the DD Sports app to notify you whenever the new content is available or to buy a subscription on time. (if you are out of one).

The feature also acts as a bookmark for shows that you might be interested in for later viewing.
