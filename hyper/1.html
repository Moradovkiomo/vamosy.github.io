Protect the little 'uns:

Of course, among Hyper's content there's stuff that's a little unsavoury for younger viewers. While you're setting up profiles, you'll see a section that says 'Allowed TV shows and movies' where you can choose between four levels of maturity (from 'little kids" to 'all').

If that's not enough control for you, you can also set up a PIN under the 'Parental controls' part in Settings.
