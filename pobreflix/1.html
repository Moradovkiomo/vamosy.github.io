What is Pobreflix Show App:

Pobreflix Show offers a wide variety of programming options in multiple languages, with a majority of content being broadcast in one of many different Indian dialects.

You can not see any of the available content on their Roku channel, however their website provides a list of current programming options.

You can subscribe through the Pobreflix Show Roku channel and follow the onscreen instructions.

If you have subscribed to the entertainment only plan, your first month is free.
