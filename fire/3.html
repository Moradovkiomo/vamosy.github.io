Free Fire Headshot Hack Using Sensitivity Settings:

Sensitivity plays the primary role in the free fire. If you are not using the proper sensitivity, it can be challenging to target the enemy’s head, so it is essential to set the right sensitivity.

Sensitivity is the touch response of your smartphone’s display. If your sensitivity is low, you need to touch smoothly on your smartphone screen, and if your sensitivity is high, you need to touch harshly on your smartphone screen.

I am sharing the best sensitivity settings for the free fire below. Apply them the same as they are mentioned below.

Open free fire game

Now click on the Settings icon on the top right corner

Now In settings, Click On the sensitivity

Now set the sensitivity as follow

General: 41

Red dot: 85

2X Scope: 23

4X Scope: 20

AWM Scope:10
