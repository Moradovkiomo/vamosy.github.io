Free Fire Headshot Settings:

Most people are using Custom HUD nowadays. Using HUD, you can customize all action buttons in free fire, But There are some HUD free fire headshot settings, which are as follows:

Open free fire game

Now click on the Settings icon on the top right corner

Now In settings, Click On Controls Option

Now Click on Custom HUD

In Custom Hud, Find the Button of Pet emotes and Place it On the center, where the Gun scoop appears

Now Change the button size to 29% and, Transparency to 49%

Now, Click On Save Free Fire Headshot Settings.

Now, You can use the pet emote Button to judge in which direction the Bullet will be going if you clicked on the fire button. This setting will help you a lot When you are using AWS Or any sniper rifle.
