Free Fire Headshot Hack Using Controls Settings:

There are some secret settings in the Control option. If you enable them, then your Aiming skills Get increase instantly.

Open free fire game

Now click on the Settings icon on the top right corner

Now In settings, Click On Controls Option

Now change some settings as Mentioned below

Lift Fire Button – Scope Only

Quick Reload – On

AWM Sniping – Hold Fire to scope

Auto Switch Gun – On

Now, these settings will work as free fire headshot hack. Just start a Match, And you will see Visible Difference in your Aiming skill, And most of the bullets fired by you will automatically connect with enemies head.
