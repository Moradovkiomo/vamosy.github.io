The Streaming Catalog

Right now it must be said that UltraFlix’s content is dominated by relatively old and niche-interest material. However, it caused a stir recently by securing the rights to stream blockbuster hit Interstellar first in 4K UHD, and it recently backed up this initial deal with Paramount with a much larger one that gives it the rights to nearly 1,000 of the studio’s film library.

Among its other 4K highlights right now are Rain Man, Fargo, The Good, The Bad And The Ugly, Rocky, and Robocop, as well as a host of concert videos and 40 IMAX titles.

The list of high profile 4K titles is growing steadily, with UltraFlix even going so far as owning a 4K post-production studio so that it can convert older films to 4K in return for a window of streaming rights exclusivity.
